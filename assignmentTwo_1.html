2.2 Laser Cutting

I uploaded my new files to Adobe Illustrator on lab's computer dedicated for laser cutter. After that I selected the objects I want to cut and change the line thickness (stroke) to 0.01 mm. This shows Epilog (the lasser cutting software) what pieces to cut. The new files looked like this:

After this I clicked Print on Adobe Illustrator and selected my printer as the Epilog Laser Cutting Software and alligned the file to thetop left corner. The print settings looked like this:

This automatically sent my file to Epilog. On Epilog I can preview my cut and I can adjust the laser cutter's vector and raster settings. I changed the settings according to the trial cuttings I used.

Before starting to laser cut, I turned on the laser cutter, compressor and the ventilation. I then put a piece of 1/8th ply wood and set a new home for the laser cutter on the ply wood. Then I focused the laser cutter using the focus option by highering the lasercutter bed. After this I went back to the computer and clicked "print" on Epilog to send my file to the laser cutter. I clicked "GO" button on laser cutter to initiate the laser cutting.

After the first file's cut (thefile withonly front and bottom sides), I put the pieces together and everything fit perfectly and that gave me a green lightto proceed with the rest of the cut. At the end I got this:
