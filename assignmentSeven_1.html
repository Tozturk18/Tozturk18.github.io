7.2 Laser Cutting and Assembling

Using Adobe Illustrator, I opened the ESP file exported from the Slicer Software. Before laser cutting, I arranged them to take less space. Unlike last time I found a better way to resize the Artboard of Adobe Illustrator to fit in the object boundaries.

After resizing the artboard, I ungrouped the items and started arranging them, I dropped the number of files to 3 after 30 min of struggle with selecting, turning and placing items.

After arranging the objects, I seperated the texts and the lines for cutting the pieces into seperate layers. I did this becausee Epilogue Laser Cutter Software does not differenciate between the lines that should be cut or engraved for this occasion.

After seperating them, I was now able to select each layer and print them seperately. This allowed me to go to Epilogue and select different Laser Cutting settings. These are the settings I chose:
