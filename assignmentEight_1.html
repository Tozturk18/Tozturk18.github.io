8.2 Preparing to Cut

This is a Vinyl Cutter:

Initially I need to select which viynl I am going to use. For this assignment because my hoodie was purple I chose to use either white or black but because I couldn't find white glitter vinyl, I used the black glitter viynl.

Before putting every thing on, I changed the settings on the vinyl cutter to fit to the vinyl type I am using. In this assignment I am using a glitter vinyl, thus according to the text on the vinyl cutter it requires 135

After adjusting the settings I placed my vinyl on to the vinyl holder behind the viynl cutter.

After putting my vinyl on the vinyl holder, I make it go through the machine while making sure that the transparent part is looking down.

After placing my vinyl in the vinyl cutter now I adjust the vinyl cutter's home point to be the corner of my vinyl and then I go to the software and press CUT. This is an image while cutting:

After the vinyl cutter finished cutting, I drop down the vinyl a bit and by using a scalpel, I cut the part from the rest of the vinyl cutter. I put back the vinyl roll back to where I found it and I take my cut vinyl on to the cutting board.

Now using a scalpel I picked one side of the vinyl and started pulling it to reveal the cut parts.

After pulling out everything it looked like this:

Now that I have the Vinyl cut out I can put it on my Hoodie and put it in the heat press.

I set the heater to 170 Celsius (350 Fahrenheit), and wait for it to warm up. When it warms up I close the lit and press it for 10 seconds.
