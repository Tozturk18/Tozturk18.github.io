3.3 Uploading to CAM

After finishing my design, I saved my file as an STL file by right clicking my object. This allowed me to transfer my file from Fusion CAD to Prusas Slicer CAM.

After opening the Prusas Slicer, I just dragged and dropped my file on to the app. After it loaded my file I checked my dimensions and adjusted it to the correct size.

After adjusting the size of my 3D object it actually became visible and it looked like this:

After making sure that the material, the printer and the size is correct, I finnally moved to computing my design acording to selected nozle with CAM. I achieved this by clicking the slice button on the bottom right corner of Prusas slicer. This gave me a sliced view which shows how the 3D printer will actually print it:

I saved this file to a SD card to move it to a 3D printer and I put a new PLA material and printed my biohazard symbol. At the end it looked like this:

I was actually happy that because the nozle was too big for the drawing, it made the top of the biohazard branches seperated so instead of a circle it looked like circles with one part missing and that is what I wanted to look like.
